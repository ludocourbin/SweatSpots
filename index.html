Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to SweatSpots, the application dedicated to helping users find outdoor gym spots. We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our application and our practices for collecting, using, and disclosing that information.

2. Data we collect

Location Data: To offer the core functionalities of SweatSpots, such as finding outdoor gym spots near you, we might access your device's location data when the app is in use. You can always turn off location sharing in your device settings. This data is not stored or shared with any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

To Provide Our Services: We use your location data to provide you with SweatSpots's services, including showing nearby outdoor gym spots and lookaround scenes.

4. Data retention

We do not retain or store any personal data from our users. The location data accessed is solely for the purpose of providing real-time services and is not stored.
